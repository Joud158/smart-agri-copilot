## Yellow spots on leaves
Possible categories:
- nutrient issue
- mite or whitefly pressure
- leaf disease
- water stress or irrigation inconsistency

## Wilting in hot afternoon only
Possible categories:
- temporary heat stress
- insufficient root-zone moisture
- root disease if wilting persists beyond heat periods

## White powder on foliage
Possible categories:
- powdery mildew likely
- confirm crop and greenhouse vs open field context

## Sticky residue on leaves
Possible categories:
- aphids
- whiteflies
- scale insects

## Clarification Prompts
The system should ask:
- Which crop is affected?
- Which growth stage?
- Open field or greenhouse?
- Is the issue patchy or uniform?
- Any visible insects, residue, or powder?
