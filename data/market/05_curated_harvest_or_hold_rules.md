## Hold is more reasonable when
- the crop stores well
- storage conditions are available
- quality loss risk is limited
- the market signal is improving gradually

## Harvest now is more reasonable when
- the crop is highly perishable
- disease pressure or weather risk is rising
- storage conditions are weak
- current quality is acceptable but may decline

## Assistant Behavior
The assistant should explain trade-offs instead of pretending certainty.
