# Simulated Seasonal Market Trend Notes

## Source
Simulated seasonal market note prepared for the Smart Agriculture Advisor demo corpus. This file is intentionally synthetic and should be described as seasonal guidance, not a live market feed.

## Tomatoes
- Prices often soften during peak local supply windows
- Quality, storage limits, and perishability reduce the value of waiting too long

## Grapes
- Short holding windows may be possible with proper cold-chain handling
- Better-quality lots may benefit more from timing and sorting than from long storage

## Olives
- Timing depends on target market use (table vs oil)
- Quality deterioration risk and processing logistics matter

## Wheat
- Market timing is less about short-term freshness and more about storage, moisture, and buyer timing

## System Rule
Recommendations should combine:
- seasonal trend direction
- storage feasibility
- crop perishability
- quality loss risk
