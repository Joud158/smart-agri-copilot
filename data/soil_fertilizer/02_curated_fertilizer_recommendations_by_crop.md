## Tomato
- Moderate to high nitrogen demand during vegetative growth
- Balanced phosphorus support is useful early
- Potassium becomes especially important around flowering and fruiting

## Cucumber
- Stable fertigation and moisture management matter
- Avoid severe over-fertilization under heat stress

## Grapes
- Excessive nitrogen can push unnecessary vegetative growth
- Balanced programs should consider vine stage and vigor

## Wheat
- Nitrogen timing matters strongly for growth and yield
- Nutrient planning should reflect rainfall, irrigation, and soil reserves

## System Rule
Any fertilizer answer must mention that exact plans should be adjusted using soil testing when available.
