## Regional Notes
### Bekaa
- Strong for field crops, vegetables, grapes, and orchards where irrigation is available
- Frost risk can matter in colder windows
- Water planning is important because summer demand can rise

### Coast
- Milder winters support vegetables, citrus, and intensive protected cultivation
- Humidity can increase disease pressure
- Salinity and irrigation-water quality may need attention in some contexts

### Mountains
- Cooler conditions can delay planting windows
- Frost and shorter warm seasons matter
- Crop choice should match elevation and exposure
