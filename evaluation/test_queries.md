# Evaluation Queries

This file is kept for quick manual demos.

The canonical structured test set is now:
- `evaluation/testset.json`

Sample manual queries:
- My tomatoes are flowering in Bekaa. Which stages are most sensitive to water deficit?
- What soil pH is generally suitable for many crops?
- My tomato leaves have white powdery patches. What issue should I suspect first?
- When is hold more reasonable for harvested produce?
- For olives, when is irrigation especially important for high yield?
